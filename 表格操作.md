## 93. 对齐表格列

```text
Name | Age | City
John | 30 | NYC
Jane | 25 | LA
```

=>

```text
Name | Age | City
-----|-----|-----
John | 30  | NYC
Jane | 25  | LA
```

## 94. 添加表格边框

```text
Name Age City
John 30  NYC
Jane 25  LA
```

=>

```text
+------+-----+------+
| Name | Age | City |
+------+-----+------+
| John | 30  | NYC  |
| Jane | 25  | LA   |
+------+-----+------+
```

## 95. 表格转 CSV

```text
| Name | Age | City |
| John | 30  | NYC  |
| Jane | 25  | LA   |
```

=>

```text
Name,Age,City
John,30,NYC
Jane,25,LA
```
